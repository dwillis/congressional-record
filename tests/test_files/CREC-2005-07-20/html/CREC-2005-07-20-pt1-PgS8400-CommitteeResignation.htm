                      COMMITTEE RESIGNATIONS

  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The Chair, on behalf of the Majority Leader,
pursuant to Public Law 106-286, appoints the following Senator to the
Commission on Security and Cooperation in Europe:

  The Senator from Tennessee (Mr. ALEXANDER).

  The PRESIDING OFFICER laid before the Senate the following letter:

                                                     U.S. Senate,
                                          Committee on Finance,
                                   Washington, DC, July 19, 2005.
Hon. Bill Frist,
Majority Leader, U.S. Senate, Washington, DC.
  Dear Mr. Leader: I hereby resign my seat on the Committee on
Finance, effective immediately.
                                                       Sincerely,
                                                  Olympia J. Snowe,
                                            U.S. Senator from Maine.

  Mr. FRIST. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the
resignation be accepted and printed in the Record.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
